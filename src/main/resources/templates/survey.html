StudentSmart

Thank you for coming here and taking the survey. This will help us a lot for improving the work on this project

This survey is about testing a web application whose aim is to provide information about and compare modules of different courses provided by UK universities to students. The application has been created to help students navigate through different university courses in one place easily without having to do separate searches in every university’s website and attempt manual evaluations whilst trying to choose which course to enrol to. The purpose of the study is to evaluate how useful and helpful the application is. The participants will be provided with a prototype of the system in order to be able to test it and explore the different features available from it. After doing so, participants will be provided with a questionnaire where they can provide feedback and evaluate the web application based on their experience with the use of it. This survey will not collect any personal data for you (e.g., your name, date of birth or address). Following the signing of the consent form, to participate in the study, you will be given a unique number (token) with which you will be able to access the system. You should keep a record of the token that you will be given so that to be able to request the deletion of the responses that you provided to the survey subsequently if you decide to do it. Hence, you will have the right to withdraw your participation at any time. You will also be able to use this token in order to be able to retrieve the outcomes of the study if you wish. The token that you will be given will be linked to the email address that you provide below until the completion of the study. Following it, the association between the token and the email address below will be deleted.
